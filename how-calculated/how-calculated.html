Happy Index - it’s our way to calculate people’s happiness in like mathematics do - in numbers. It base on four main characteristics for each country: nature, infrastructure, judge and zero hunger.

HappyIndex = (Zero Hunger + Nature + Judge + Infrastructure) / 4
Nature = (CO2 Emmission + Forest Index + Number of Persons affected by disaster per 100,000 people
Proportion of important sites for freshwater biodiversity + Proportion of important sites for terrstrival biodiversity + Red list) / 6
Infrastructure = Logistics Performance Index
Judge = (Crime Index of Country + Deaths) / 2
